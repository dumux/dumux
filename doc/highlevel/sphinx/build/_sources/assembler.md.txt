## assembler

|The assembler is used to calculate the global Jacobian-matrix and the global residual vector. </br> In order to do so, it uses a discretization specific localAssembler. </br> The assembler creates a shared_ptr of the Jacobian-matrix and a shared_ptr of the residual.|
| :--- |

### Key functionalites

1. assembleJacobianAndResidual()
   1. Calls the assembleJacobianAndResidual() function of the {ref}`localAssembler`.
